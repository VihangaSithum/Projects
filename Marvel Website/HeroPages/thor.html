THOR

THE GOD OF THUNDER

The character THOR, which is based on the Norse deity of the same name, is the Asgardian god of thunder who possesses the enchanted hammer, Mjolnir, which grants him the ability to fly and manipulate weather amongst his other superhuman attributes.

The Germanic Mythology that inspired MARVEL character THOR

Thor's father Odin decides his son needed to be taught humility and consequently places Thor into the body and memories of an existing, partially disabled human medical student, Donald Blake. After becoming a doctor, Blake witnesses the arrival of an alien scouting party while he is on vacation in Norway. Blake flees from the aliens into a cave. After discovering Thor's hammer Mjolnir and striking it against a rock, he transforms into the thunder god. Later, in Thor, Blake is revealed to have always been Thor, Odin's enchantment having caused him to forget his history as The Thunder God and believe himself mortal.
